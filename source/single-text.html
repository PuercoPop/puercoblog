Dijkstra on Analogies

Recently a great Twitter account I follow, Symbol1cs Ideas posted a link to a speech from Edward Dijkstra regarding the teaching of Computer Science. Dijkstra makes a point that there is even another reason as to avoid reasoning by analogy. We can concepts that make sense in the previous abstraction can leak into the current one. Dijkstra uses the idea of Bug. But for example, homeopathy dependend on the infinite divisibilty of matter, a notion modern science now rejects. But for example, why do we asume that space is infinitelly divisible just because we use numbers to measure it? (Funfact: Arquimides rejected the the notion of infinitesimal numbers).
